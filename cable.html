What cable provider do I want?


Pricing

  • Spectrum

    • Spectrum cable TV is the cheapest provider in this U.S., but it is surprisingly expensive. According to a Consumerwatch.org analysis in March, 2019, the rates can go sky high and it may even be more “pricy.” In fact, it may not even be on this list.


  • FiOS

    FIOS may seem expensive, but the picture quality is much, much better than that of other cable providers.
