Troubleshooting

Here are some common issues and solutions:

  • No signal: Check that your dish is properly aligned and all cables are securely connected.
  • Pixelated or freezing image: Try resetting your receiver or contacting customer support.
  • Unknown error message: Refer to the troubleshooting guide in your user manual or contact customer support.

FAQ

  • How do I add premium channels to my package?
  • How do I program my remote control?
  • How can I pay my bill?

How do I add premium channels to my package?

You can add premium channels to your package by logging into your account on the Dish TV website or by contacting customer support.

How do I program my remote control?

You can program your remote control by following the instructions in your user manual or by visiting the Dish TV website.

How can I pay my bill?

You can pay your bill online, by phone, or by mail. Visit the Dish TV website for more information.
