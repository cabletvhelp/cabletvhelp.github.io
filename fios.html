Fios TV Help

Troubleshooting

If you're experiencing issues with your Fios TV service, try the following steps:

  • Ensure that your TV and Fios TV Set-Top Box are powered on
  • Check all cables to ensure they are securely connected
  • Reset your Set-Top Box by unplugging it for 30 seconds, then plugging it back in

Contact Support
