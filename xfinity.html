Internet Troubleshooting

If you are experiencing issues with your Xfinity internet service, try the following steps:

  • Restart your modem and router
  • Check for outages in your area
  • Ensure your cables are securely connected
  • Try connecting to the internet using a different device

TV Troubleshooting

If you are experiencing issues with your Xfinity TV service, try the following steps:

  • Ensure your cables are securely connected
  • Check for outages in your area
  • Try restarting your cable box
  • Ensure your TV is set to the correct input

Phone Troubleshooting

If you are experiencing issues with your Xfinity phone service, try the following steps:

  • Ensure your phone is properly connected to the wall jack
  • Check for outages in your area
  • Try restarting your phone
  • Ensure your phone is set to the correct setting

Contact Us

If you are still experiencing issues, please contact Xfinity customer service at 1-800-XFINITY.
