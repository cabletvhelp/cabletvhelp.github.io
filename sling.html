Getting Started

Welcome to Sling TV! Here are a few tips to help you get started:

  • Make sure you have a high-speed internet connection.
  • Download and install the Sling TV app on your device.
  • Sign in with your account information.
  • Choose your package and start watching!

Troubleshooting

If you're experiencing any issues with Sling TV, try the following:

  • Restart your device and try again.
  • Check your internet connection and try restarting your router.
  • Make sure you have the latest version of the Sling TV app.
  • Try clearing the app's cache and data.
